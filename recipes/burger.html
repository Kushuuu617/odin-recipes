Burger

Description

A burger is a timeless favorite, perfect for quick meals and backyard barbecues. Made with a juicy patty, melted cheese, and fresh toppings, it's both satisfying and customizable.

This crispy cheese burger recipe adds an extra crunch with perfectly toasted buns and golden cheese edges, delivering restaurant-style flavor right at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 burger buns
  • 2 beef patties (or plant-based alternatives)
  • 2 slices of cheese (Comté, cheddar, or your favorite)
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 2 slices tomato
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Butter or oil for toasting buns

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat.
  2. Season the patties with salt and pepper, then cook for 3–4 minutes per side or until done to your liking.
  3. Place a slice of cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  4. Butter the buns and toast them on the skillet until golden brown.
  5. Assemble the burger: spread mayo on the bottom bun, add lettuce, patty with cheese, tomato slice, and pickles if desired.
  6. Top with the other bun, press gently, and serve hot.
