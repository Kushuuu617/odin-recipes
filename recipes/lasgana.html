Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort food layered with sheets of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and gooey melted cheese. It's a hearty dish perfect for family dinners and special occasions.

This version combines ground beef, tomato sauce, and a mix of ricotta and mozzarella cheese to create a flavorful and satisfying meal that’s baked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté garlic. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Stir in marinara sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, then noodles, then ricotta, mozzarella, and repeat layers.
  6. Top with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake another 10-15 minutes until cheese is golden and bubbly.
  8. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.
