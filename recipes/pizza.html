Pizza

Description

Pizza is one of the most beloved dishes worldwide, known for its crispy crust, flavorful tomato sauce, gooey cheese, and a variety of toppings. This classic cheese pizza is simple yet irresistibly delicious.

Whether you're cooking for a party or a cozy night in, homemade pizza is fun to make and can be customized to suit any taste preference. This recipe delivers a perfect balance of flavors and textures.

Ingredients

  • 1 prepared pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese generously on top.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle oregano and salt.
  7. Bake for 12–15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from oven, top with fresh basil if using, slice, and serve hot.
